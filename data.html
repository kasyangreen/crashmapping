The data set is developed and maintained by the Massachusetts Vision Zero Coalition. Pedestrian fatalities are collected based on media reports by WalkBoston. Bicycle fatalities are collected based on media accounts by MassBike. The full dataset is hosted by Livable Streets Alliance. Currently, the Fatal Data set includes 440 instances with up to 9 attributes covering the years 2016 through 2021. The attributes are: Date of crash, Town/City, Time of crash, Name, Age, Type (pedestrian or bicyclist), Link to media report, Longitude coordinate, and Latitude coordinate.
