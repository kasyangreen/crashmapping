About Us

Tom Francis (he/him)

Tom is a MS-GIS major interested in the built environment and climate resiliency. He was introduced to the Vision Zero Coalition of Massachusetts through his work with MassBike. If he's not on his computer or riding his bike, you can probably find him in the kitchen.

Zainab Farid (she/her)

Hailing from Pakistan, Zainab identifies as a storyteller who uses quantitative and qualitative research, filmmaking, GIS and relationship-building to promote equality and justice in sustainable ways. Aspirations include employing her system thinking skills to contribute to the world of conservation, social innovation and analytical research.
